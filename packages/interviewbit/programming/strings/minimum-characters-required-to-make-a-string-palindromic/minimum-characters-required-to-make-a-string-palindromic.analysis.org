| Gia su input la "banana" ta can lam gi ?                   | Ta can them chuoi 'X' vao "banana" de cho "Xbanana" la palindrome                                                                                                                     |

| Lam sao de ~"Xbanana" === "ananabR"~                       | Neu ta dat "banana" va "ananab" tren va duoi, thi ta can slide "ananab" sao cho 2 "b" trung nhau thi ta moi co the tao duoc palindrome string                                         |
